Welcome!
WE ARE SCOGEM ENTERPRISES LIMITED

Scogem Enterprises limited was incorporated as a limited liability company on 3rd May 2013.

  • Main businesses Activity:

    The Company’s Main business attribute is Commercial Supply and Technical Support Service to agro-processing machines and equipment for seeds, coffee, and other grains.

  • SCOGEM is franchised to supply equipment from the following principles:

    • Penagos Hermanos & CIA Ltda., Colombia/ Brazil S/A - Coffee Mills for the Primary, Intermediary, and Dry Mill stages. (Pulping machines, Dryers and Hulling and Grading lines).

    • Satake Corporation, Japan - Electronic Color Sorters.

    • Yargus Corporation Inc., Illinois USA - Technical support to NPK Fertilizer blending plants/ facilities.

  • SCOGEM also supplies coffee laboratory equipment, Commercial Roasters, Grinders, and Packaging machines for coffee, Moisture meters, Storage Silos, Rice mills, seed planters, Air Compressors for agro-processing plants, Maize/ Corn Sheller (Thresher), Forage Shredders/ Choppers, and Feed Mills, Maize Flour Mills for human consumption.
  • The company has a competent technical department that offers installation and maintenance services in line with its range of supplies.
  • We are dedicated to training technical staff and operators of clients for whom our machines are installed. Training entails product knowledge, operations, routine maintenance, basic troubleshooting, and repair skills.
  • It is a prime provision by the company to offer a full package of post-sales technical and commercial services to all clients within Uganda. SCOGEM has extended cross boarder service support to neighboring countries. 
  • On behalf of our Principal manufacturers, we are authorized to commission installations duly completed
