Who We Are

We are Scogem Enterprises Limited.

Born in May 2013, SCOGEM Enterprises Limited is an award-winning agro-based company transforming the lives of farmers in domestic and commercial agricultural practices in Uganda, Africa, and the world over. We deal with corporate companies' industries in the Agro-processing sector, importing and supplying top brands of machines that ease and quicken agro-processing.

SCOGEM is an acronym to mean Seed, Coffee & Grain Equipment, and Machinery. Consequently, what we want to see is that the agricultural industry takes on the evolving trends in innovative technology thus better-refined end products without value and content distortion. The company has had and continues to have unprecedented and unparalleled growth and expansion. History has recorded that in only six years of her existence, SCOGEM has already won an award for best and accredited Agro-processing Machinery supplier, by The Uganda National Farmersâ€™ Federation at the July 2019 annual Agricultural show.
